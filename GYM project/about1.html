About Us

For over 15 years, 24/7 Fitness Club has been one of the local leaders in wellness, fitness and the management of fitness centers and leisure facilities. We pride ourselves as the leading innovators, by being the first to implement 24/7 opening hours, physiotherapists as part of our team, free group classes, and advocating training ethics within all our outlets. Our company embraces the values of professionalism, flexibility, commitment & client focus.
Our mission is to make a positive change to people's lives
It's our belief that with some encouragement and setting of realistic goals, anyone coming to our clubs can feel better, have fun and minimize stress, hence enhancing their way of life.
It is our vision to provide clubs that help people get fit and feel great in a friendly and welcoming atmosphere, helped by friendly staff, and using state of the art facilities.
